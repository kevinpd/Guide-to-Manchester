Bierkeller

Cheap, cheerful and an amazing open mic night!

What Can You Find at Bierkeller ?

Every Tuesday from 8pm Bierkeller host an open mic night for talented artists to come and perform and you even get to vote your favourite artist of the night. Bierkeller also host a live Oompah band show every Friday night. If you're looking for a lively and entertaining night of live music then Bierkeller is the place to be for you.

If you're a student then you can take advantage of their student loyalty card. Just ask the staff for details.
